ABOUT US

We are REFILLING OIL AND GAS LIMITED, a Department of Petroleum Resources (DPR) Licensed Operator of DIESEL REFILL -AUTOMOTIVE GAS OIL (AGO) MOBILE DISPENSING. We are an emerging petroleum products marketer and haulage service delivery company with Headquarter and Sales Office in Lagos, Nigeria. We commenced operations in October, 2018 and now operate a network of Diesel sales and haulage delivery services in Ogun State, Edo State, Delta State and Rivers State as more states would be added soon.
